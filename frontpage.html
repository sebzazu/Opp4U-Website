OPP4U

Run by Student-Athletes, for Student-Athletes

Our Mission

OPP4U's mission is to connect collegiate athletes of all sports and backgrounds with brands and business for brand deals and sponsorships.

It all started in June 2021 when the NCAA allowed athletes to profit off of their NIL (Name, Image, Likeness). Prior to the new NIL laws, athletes were prohibited from profiting off of their NIL while playing for their college. With NIL laws now in place, athletes can negotiate sponsorships and endorsement deals, receive compensation for social media promotions, and monetize their personal brands. This process is not easy for all athletes because players at big name schools (ex: Alabama Football) typically have managers and support teams. This is a far different experience for, say, a softball player at a smaller Divison 1 program. This is where OPP4U comes in. We want to level the playing field for all athletes, so they can get the sponsorship they deserve

How It Works

A seamless approach for the busy user

Student-athletes enter their basic information into our database.. Using this information, sponsors reach out to these athletes if they are a good fit for the sponsor's brand. Prospective Sponsors can create an account here.. This approach is simple, and everyone gets what their looking for. Athletes get exposure they otherwise wouldn't be getting. Sponsors get a list of possible suitors to promote their brand.
